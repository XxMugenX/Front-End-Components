TOTAL TIME
10 minutes

Sweet Vanilla Iced Coffee is easy to make and stores well in the fridge. Make a large batch and save yourself a few trips to the coffee shop

INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Boil some water and let it set for 1-2 minutes
  2. Put the coffee powder into a cafetiere and gently pour in the water
  3. Let the coffee steep for 5 minutes then slowly press down the plunger on your cafetiere
  4. Pour the coffee into a glass, add the vanilla syrup then stir to combine
  5. Add ice to chill the coffee. Pour your favorite cream or coffee creamer into the drink and stir gently.
  6. Taste the coffee to see if it needs more cream or simple syrup. Serve cold and enjoy!
  7. The coffee can be stored in the fridge for up to 5 days. shake and stir again before serving
